The Hidden Harms of NSCLC

Lung Damage & Physical Symptoms

  • Persistent cough, shortness of breath
  • Chest pain, fatigue, weight loss

Metastasis – How Cancer Spreads

  • NSCLC can spread from the lungs
  • to brain, bones, liver, etc.

Impact on Quality of Life

  • Reduced physical activity & energy
  • Mental health challenges
  • Social isolation & financial strain

Statistics & Hard Facts

  • 80–85% of lung cancers are NSCLC
  • Over 2.2M new cases worldwide annually
  • 5-year survival: up to 55% early, below 15% advanced
  • Nearly 1.8M deaths per year globally
  • Emerging therapies slowly improving outcomes

Early Detection & Transformation Risks

  • Early screenings improve outcomes
  • Prevent complications like small cell transformation
